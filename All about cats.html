fun facts about cats

  • A cats hearing is better than a dogs. And a cat can hear high-frequency
    sounds up to two octaves higher than a human.
  • A cat can travel at a top speed of approximately
    31 mph (49 km) over a short distance.
  • A cats brain is biologically more similar to a human brain
    than it is to a dogs. Both humans and cats have identical regions
    in their brains that are responsible for emotions.
